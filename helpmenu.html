In this game, your character is a dog, trying to avoid bath time, your objective is to run
as long as you can, and avoid the dog kennel traps. To play this game you only need
space bar, to make the dog jump. By each 25meters you run, you'll earn a coin, that you
can later use to buy distance boosts, that make you start ahead of the regular distance!
You can control the music volume on the settings.
You now know everything you need to play this game! Good luck, and most important, have fun!
